# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We want RegressProof to be a respectful, practical, and evidence-focused project.

Contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to make this community welcoming and safe for everyone, regardless of experience level, identity, background, or viewpoint.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Assume good intent, but be clear and honest.
- Prefer evidence, reproducibility, and concrete examples.
- Help keep discussions useful for contributors and users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, abuse, or hateful conduct.
- Personal attacks or insulting language.
- Publishing private or sensitive information without permission.
- Bad-faith disruption, intimidation, or repeated antagonistic behavior.
- Spam or off-topic promotion.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, pull requests, and other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

Serious or repeated violations may result in temporary or permanent restrictions from project spaces.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact the repository owner through GitHub and include:

- what happened
- where it happened
- relevant links or screenshots
- any context that helps reproduction or review

Please avoid posting sensitive reports in public issues when privacy is important.
